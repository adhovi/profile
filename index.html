Who I Am

About Me

I am a graduate civil engineer with a strong foundation in structural engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) — one of Bangladesh's most prestigious technical institutions. My academic journey spanned structural analysis, seismic evaluation, steel and concrete design, and computational modelling.

My work spans from designing retail complexes in Dhaka to analyzing core wall buildings under seismic loads per BNBC 2020. I am proficient with industry-standard tools including ETABS, SAP2000, and AutoCAD, and I bring programming skills in Python, MATLAB, and C++ to computational challenges.

Beyond academics, I served as a leader in BUET's Innovation & Designing Club and Math Club, and I'm driven by the ambition to build a career delivering resilient, efficient structures for the next generation of Bangladesh's infrastructure.

Undergraduate Thesis

CE 400: Project & Thesis · BUET, 2023

Structural Performance of RC Core Wall Buildings with and without Moment Resisting Frames

Investigated the feasibility of reducing or eliminating moment-resisting frames by substituting a core shear wall system — a design strategy with significant implications for architectural space efficiency and structural economy.
